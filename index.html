Welcome to Bathroom Finder, this intuitive application allows users to locate bathrooms around UIC since the campus is so wide and contains so many unique buldings around the city.

Instructions:
  • Select markers on the map to see more detail about bathroom location.
  • Use your device to find your locatin to see the closest bathroom to you.
  • Add a bathroom to your personal favorites for future references.

Andy Li is an independent developer, his github can be access by clicking on the developer profile picture.
